# 🎯 PROJECT SCOPE

The **Customer Churn Prediction Dashboard** is designed to analyze customer subscription behavior and predict whether a customer is likely to leave a service.

The project leverages **Data Analytics**, **Machine Learning**, and **Interactive Dashboards** to help organizations improve customer retention and reduce churn.

### Key Objectives

✅ Analyze customer behavior

✅ Identify churn patterns

✅ Predict customer attrition

✅ Improve customer retention strategies

✅ Support business decision-making

---

# 🚀 PROJECT OVERVIEW

Customer churn is one of the most critical business challenges faced by subscription-based companies.

This project uses historical customer data to:

- Understand customer behavior
- Identify factors contributing to churn
- Predict future churn risk
- Support retention campaigns
